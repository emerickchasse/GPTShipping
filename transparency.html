Facts before checkout

What is verified — and what is not open yet.

This page records the customer-facing launch rules for Pet Parade. It will be revised before orders open whenever supplier, market, payment, or support details change.

Current status

No orders, payments, shipping addresses, customer accounts, or email signups are accepted on this preview. The private support form accepts only information submitted for a support request. A disabled checkout control is not an invitation to order.

Sizing

Three square sizes, with an important limit

S, M, and L are configured with the supplier. Its current size guide lists equal length and width:

Supplier size guide
SizeLengthWidth
S17⅜″ / 44 cm17⅜″ / 44 cm
M21¼″ / 54 cm21¼″ / 54 cm
L25¼″ / 64 cm25¼″ / 64 cm

The supplier states that S is intended for small pets and will not fit an adult; adults should use M or L. Fit still depends on how the square is folded and tied.

Shipping

Markets stay closed until verified

The US supplier base cost and standard one-item shipping rate have been verified. Delivery estimates can change and are not guarantees. A destination-specific estimate, tax treatment, tracking method, and any import-cost warning must be shown before an order is accepted.

Returns

Made-to-order limits must be clear

The supplier may replace or refund items that arrive damaged or have a manufacturing error when evidence is submitted within 30 days. Its policy does not cover buyer’s remorse or a customer choosing the wrong size or colour. PawSwipe has not opened sales and has not yet adopted a final customer return policy.

Privacy

No storefront tracking or payment forms

This preview does not run PawSwipe analytics, advertising pixels, customer accounts, email signup, or checkout. The optional private support form is hosted by Google and collects a help category, reply email, optional PawSwipe order reference, message, and privacy confirmation only when submitted. Never include a full card number, card security code, password, one-time code, government ID, or unrelated personal data.

Product

Verified construction facts

The saved supplier template uses an original PawSwipe repeat pattern, a single-sided all-over synthetic print, double-folded edges, black stitching, and a tear-away care label. Material composition varies by fulfilment region. Sample quality has not yet been inspected by PawSwipe.

Support

A private, monitored support route

Use the PawSwipe private support form for product, policy, privacy, or future order help. New responses notify the monitored PawSwipe support queue. PawSwipe targets an initial review within one US business day during the pilot, without promising a resolution before records are verified. Never submit card details, passwords, one-time codes, government IDs, or unrelated personal data.

Last reviewed July 12, 2026

Transparency is a launch gate, not decoration.

If checkout is closed, no customer should send money or personal information through another channel claiming to represent this preview.
