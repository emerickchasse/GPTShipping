The PawSwipe care guide

A gentler reset for the pet-loving home.

A few small habits can make the everyday fur situation feel more manageable — without turning your home into a constant cleaning project.

01

Give high-traffic spots a quick reset

Keep your favourite clean-up tool near the sofa, wardrobe, or door. A brief touch-up is often easier than saving everything for a big weekend clean.

02

Work with the fabrics you use most

Notice which surfaces gather visible hair fastest in your home. A simple routine tailored to those places helps you spend less energy chasing every stray strand.

03

Keep the ritual kind

Pet hair is part of sharing a home with an animal you love. The goal is not perfection — it is making space for comfort, company, and an easier daily reset.
