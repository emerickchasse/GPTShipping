Before you choose

Pet Parade questions, answered plainly.

Is Pet Parade a tie-on or over-collar bandana?

Pet Parade is a square tie-on bandana. It folds into a triangle and does not have a sewn over-collar channel. Compare the two constructions.

How do I choose a size?

Compare a square bandana your pet already wears comfortably with S 44 cm, M 54 cm, and L 64 cm before folding and tying. Size labels are not universal. Open the verified size guide.

Are orders open now?

No. Checkout remains closed while PawSwipe verifies the physical sample, taxes, eligible markets, customer policies, private support, and payment configuration. See the current launch gates.

Does the bandana replace a collar or restraint?

No. It is an accessory only. It does not replace a collar, identification, harness, or restraint, and it should be used with supervision.
